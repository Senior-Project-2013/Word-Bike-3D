The Legend of Wop
Long ago, in a galaxy far, far away, a creature named Wop roamed a cube planet.
Wop was all alone :'( It walked around this planet for centuries. Wop soon became bored.
In an attempt to relieve some boredom, Wop spent all its free time learning how to spell. It became the greatest speller in all of the universe.
One day while brushing its hair, Wop noticed a large tumor growing off the back of its head.
It turns out the tumor was actually an exact copy of itself. However, the copy was a terrible speller. Wop was very surprised to learn that it was asexual!
Soon the cube was inhabited by a plethora of these creatures, now named "Worphles". All these Worphles claimed to be the original Wop!
The Worphles became violent. Everyone wanted to be acknowledged as the first Worphle.
The Worphles realized this bloodshed was unnecessary. They could instead use a vital fact about Wop. Wop was a great speller!
A spelling contest was organized. The Worphles made their planet into a playing field for this competition.
The competition continues even today. The winner will be crowned Wop, ruler of words and Worphles! ;)
