FAQ

  • So, what is Word Bike 3D?
    Word Bike 3D is a word game...no, a word stringing EXPERIENCE unlike no other. Players are challenged to think outside their mortal 2D senses, and instead, to EXPERIENCE a challenging and competitive 3D word stringing environment. If this doesn't answer your question, play EXPERIENCE it.
  • Who were the revolutionists that developed this EXPERIENCE?
    Five college students at Grand Valley State University.
